Methodology

This verification follows the Gold Standard methodology for quantifying and monitoring greenhouse gas emission reductions from clean cooking solutions.

  • Baseline: Traditional cooking methods (charcoal, wood, kerosene)
  • Project: Clean cooking devices (electric, induction, LPG)
  • Monitoring: IoT-enabled usage tracking and fuel displacement calculation
  • Calculation: Emission factors applied to fuel displacement data

Finding 1: Usage Data Discrepancy

Low

Updated emission factors for charcoal production in Kenya resulted in a slight reduction in calculated emissions savings.

Info

Recommendation to improve the monitoring system for devices in areas with weak connectivity to reduce data gaps.
